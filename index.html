What is WorldFlows?

WorldFlows is a travel-first platform where you can create practical plans, save ideas, and personalize your journey based on your interests. It is built for people who want less chaos and better travel decisions.

What is Zelo?

Zelo is the social layer inside WorldFlows. You can create a meetup intent, share a location, and connect with others who have similar plans and vibe.

Listing Token

Use one listing token to create your own Zelo place.

Joinable Token

Use one joinable token to join a Zelo place created by someone else.

How to Use WorldFlows

  1. Choose your destination and travel preferences.
  2. Generate and save your trip plan.
  3. Open Zelo and create or join meetups.
  4. Chat with people and coordinate safely.

Privacy Policy (Quick View)

We only use data needed to run travel planning and social features. We do not sell personal data. You can control privacy settings in the app at any time.
